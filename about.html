About us

I feel privileged to be a part of S.R. Group of school committee. It is a matter of pride to pen down the message for the website. My heart is filled with immense pleasure as I perceive the progress being made by the school. We just believe in holistic education for your children encompassing academics, co-curricular activities, sports education and life skills learning. I request the parents to spare sometime with their ward regularly to monitor their performance in studies as well as in other activities. They should provide ample study atmosphere at home by restricting limited time for television, mobile and internet. The students and parents of the school are very fortunate as most of the students passed out of the school are placed in prestigious organisations in various field and settled in India and Abroad.

My sincere advice to all the students that they have to work hard to face the global competition to survive. I request the teachers to maintain the team spirit and continue their efforts in giving the best to the students and to make them noble citizens of India. I extend my warm wishes to principal, staff and students of school to continue the journey on the road of excellence.

Best Wishes
Mr Ronjoy kumar
